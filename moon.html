Why Moon?

That floating feeling...

We chose the name "Moonball" because of the way the ball floats when it's spinning through the air.

As humans come closer to returning to the Moon with NASA's Artemis missions, the name "Moonball" creates a natural touchpoint to connect the excitement up there to the world down here.

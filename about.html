About Moonball

The Game

Moonball is the ideal combination of ball striking, ball spin and ball speed. The ball comes off the hand (or foot) with terrific speed, but because of its weight, it slows before it reaches the end of the court, and it spins in a very pleasing way.

Because a player can use their feet, it removes the danger of leaning over to play a low ball with a paddle.

Good players will use their right hand and their left hand equally, as well as their feet (or head).

The Ball

Hedstrom makes the best ball. It should be lightweight (around 100 grams), appoximately the size of a volleyball with a stiff yet bouncy skin.
